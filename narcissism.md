# Confidence vs. Narcissism — 怎么养出一个自信但不自恋的孩子 

**A "tripartite model" — self-esteem without narcissism can be cultivated through three things:**

**1. realistic feedback (rather than inflated praise)**

情景：Your kid draws a picture. Don't say "这是我见过最好看的画！" (inflated praise). Say "我看到你用了很多颜色，这边这个细节很有意思" (specific, realistic, process-focused). The first one teaches him "I am special"; the second teaches him "what I did has value."

**2. a focus on growth (rather than on outperforming others)**

情景：在中文语境下，这一点尤其重要。很多中国家长的夸奖模式是"你真聪明"（fixed trait praise），或者反过来，骂孩子"你怎么这么笨"。两者都是在给孩子的智力贴标签。更好的做法是关注过程："你这道题想了很久，最后换了个方法试，结果做出来了"——这教会孩子的是"困难不代表我不行，而是我还没找到对的方法"。

**3. unconditional regard (rather than conditional regard)**

情景：Practically: he fails a test, he comes home upset. Your response isn't "没关系，你最棒了" (inflated, dismissive). It's also not "你怎么考这么差" (your parents' path). It's something like: "考得不好确实不舒服。你觉得哪里出了问题？我们看看下次可以怎么调整。" You're communicating: I see you're struggling, I'm here, and I believe you can figure this out — without tying his worth as a person to the number on the test.
